Progate

Progate is an online platform for beginners to learn programming. It offers a fully equipped coding environment to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Java, Python, and more. Started in Japan, Progate has over 1.5 million learners from 100+ countries.
